About Everyday Kibera Tours

From the tradional tours experience, Everyday Kibera avails an opportunity to not only visit but also experience the true feeling the community through culture, sports and customs.

We are community members working and living in Kibera, with us, your safety and all your materials are safe with us. There is no maximum number .

We are knowledgeable tour guides eager to share our personal experiences and how it relates to our environment. We share a passion for our community and the stories we share from here. .
