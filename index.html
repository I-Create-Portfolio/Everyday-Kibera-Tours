Everyday Kibera slum Tours

Everyday Kibera slum tours avails an opportunity to not only visit, but also experience the community through its culture, sports, customs...

We are community members working and living in Kibera slums. With us "Everyday Kibera Tours", your safety and all your luggage are safe with us.

We are knowledgeable tour guides and also passionate about sharing our community's stories and personal experiences, and how it relates to the environment.
